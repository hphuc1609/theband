BTS, also known as Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 by Big Hit Entertainment. The group consists of 7 members: Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V and Jungkook. The members co-wrote and produced most of their music.
The famous saying of BTS is “WooJiHwa”. WooJiHwa stands for "WooRi HamKkaeHaNeun JiGeum, HwaYangYeonHwa.", which means "The time we spent together is the happiest time of my life". HwaYangYeonHwa is a 4-letter Chinese phrase used to express being at the happiest moment in life and is also the name of the mini-album that made BTS's name. It really makes a lot of sense! And in response, ARMY (BTS's fandom name) quoted the saying "Forever your audience" to show their loyalty to BTS oppas!.
